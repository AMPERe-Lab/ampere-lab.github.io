## Open Positions

We are always looking to recruit motivated and passionate members to join our team. We work on exciting problems at the intersection of electric machine design, power electronics, and physics-based control — with real hardware demonstrations and strong connections to industry and national labs.

---

## PhD Students

We have **openings for PhD students** starting **Fall 2026** and beyond.

**We are looking for students with:**
- A completed B.S. or M.S. in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or a closely related field.
- Strong fundamentals in electric machines, power electronics, circuits, control systems, or a related area.
- Hands-on experience with hardware and a passion to build things.
- Interest in at least one of: electric machine design, power electronics, optimization, or AI/ML applied to engineering

**What you will get:**
- Full funding (tuition + stipend) through research assistantship.
- Access to state-of-the-art laboratory and computing resources at Notre Dame.
- Mentorship and close collaboration with the PI.
- Opportunities to collaborate with external partners.
- A strong publication track record and preparation for academic or industry careers.

**To apply:**
Please fill this google form. e-mails will not be considered.

You should also apply formally through the [Notre Dame EE PhD program](https://engineering.nd.edu/graduate-programs/graduate-admissions/) and mention Prof. Gadiyar in your application.
