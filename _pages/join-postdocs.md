- A limited number of funded post-doc positions are available. If you have a strong publication record and hands-on research experience in electric machines, power electronics, or related areas, please fill this [Google form](https://forms.gle/hhWePcefS7kY8JMF8).
- We are also able to host you if you are funded through post-doc fellowships / external grants.
- Notre Dame also has a prestigious provost's post-doctoral fellowship. If interested, please review [this page](https://engineering.nd.edu/provosts-postdoctoral-fellowships-in-engineering-program/) and get in touch with Dr. Gadiyar. The application deadline is typically in early December.
