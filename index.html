AMPERE Lab

Welcome to the Advanced Electric Machines and Power Electronics Research (AMPERE) Laboratory at the University of Notre Dame. We design, analyze, and control high-performance electro-mechanical systems — advancing sustainable energy conversion for energy generation, electric vehicles, aerospace, actuators, and industrial systems. We operate at the intersection of the traditional areas of electric machines, power electronics, and control, with a soft spot for highly integrated, cool, and "weird" designs that push the performance envelope.
